Lasagna

A quick approach to meat lasagna

This is a recipe for those that do not have willpower to spare on fancy instructions and presentation. you will be given a time optimised recipe that will satisfy you tastebuds and ignore the visual since you dont need to look at your food while eating and coding at the same time.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs. (15 mL) olive oil
  • 1 large (300 g) onion, chopped fine
  • 6 large (25 g) garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 lb. (225 g) ground beef
  • 1/2 lb. (225 g) mild italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1/2 tsp. (3 g) salt
  • 1/2 tsp. (1.2 g) ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup (60 mL) heavy cream
  • 28-29 oz. (794-822 g) puree tomatoes
  • 28-29 oz. (794-822 g) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 15 oz. (425 g) ricotta cheese
  • 1-1/4 cup (100 g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1-1/4 cup (100 g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large (50 g) egg, beaten
  • 1/2 tsp. (3 g) salt
  • 1/2 tsp. (1.2 g) ground black pepper
  • 12 oven ready lasagna pasta
  • 16 oz. (455 g) mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Sauce Phase

    1. Heat a large pan or pot over medium flame
    2. Pour 1 tablespoon oil into the heated pan
    3. After it begins to shimmer, put the finely chopped onions into the pan
    4. Cook for about two minutes, stirring occasionally, until the onions have soften
    5. Add garlic and cook for another two minutes
    6. Add the ground meat and increase heat to medium-high
    7. Add salt and pepper to taste
    8. Cook until the meat loses it's pink color, but not start to brown
    9. Stir in 1/4 cup heavy cream
    10. Allow the mixture to simmer and thicken
    11. Add a can of pureed tomatoes and a can of diced tomatoes (drained)
    12. After bubbles begin to form, lower the heat to low and cook for a few more minutes

  2. Layers Phase

    1. put the ricotta cheese, a cup of parmesan (reserving 1/4 cup for use later), the chopped basil, beaten egg, and some salt and pepper into a medium bowl for mixing.
    2. Mix the ricotta and flavorings together with a fork

  3. Impementation Phase

    1. Spoon the ricotta mix unto each pasta piece - about two tablespoons each. Distribute any excess evenly and flatten the ricotta onto each pasta piece.
    2. Apply a thin layer of meat sauce to the bottom of the baking pan
    3. Place three lasagna pasta (with the ricotta facing up) into the pan
    4. Cover the pasta with a fourth of the shredded mozzarella cheese
    5. Spoon enough meat sauce to cover the mozzarella and place another layer of lasgna pasta
    6. Repeat with mozzarella, meat sauce, lasagna, mozzarella, and meat sauce
    7. The top layer of pasta goes on the meat sauce, upside down
    8. Cover this layer with the remaining sauce
    9. Cover the top layer of red sauce with the remaining mozzarella cheese and then sprinkle the final 1/4 cup of parmesan on top

  4. Baking Phase

    1. brush or spray some oil onto the foil
    2. Cover the top with aluminum foil
    3. Place the lasanga onto the middle rack in a preheated 375F (190C) oven for 15 minutes
    4. Remove the foil and continue baking for 25 more minutes

  5. Serving Phase

    1. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes
    2. Cut and serve 6 large portions
